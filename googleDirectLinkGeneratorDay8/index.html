Step-By-Step Instructions to get the link to your Google Drive file

  1. Open Google Drive

  2. Navigate to the preferred file.

  3. Right click on the preferred file.

  4. Select "Get Link" and click "Copy Link"

  5. Paste the link in the placeholder and click the "Generate Direct Download Link" button.
